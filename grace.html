Women in Computing

Grace Hopper

Grace Hopper was a pioneer not only in computer science, she joined the US Navy Reserve at the age of 34, with a special exemption due to being 15 pounds underweight. She invented one of the first compiler related tools. Thanks to Hopper, we can write code in English and compilers will convert it into machine code that computers can understand.
